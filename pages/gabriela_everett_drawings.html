Gabriela Everett is a multi-disciplinary artist based in her hometown of Las Vegas. She posesses a BA in creative writing from Columbia College Chicago and an affinity for coffee at midnight. Everett's previous publications include fiction and prose in Mulberry Literary, Columbia College of Chicago’s Hair Trigger, and Santa Fe University of Art and Design's lit mag, Glyph. Her visual artwork can be found on instagram, @mangosherbet.
