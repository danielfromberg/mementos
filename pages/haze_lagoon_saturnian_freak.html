Saturnian Freak

By Haze Lagoon



Haze Lagoon · Saturnian Freak

Haze Lagoon has released the teaser "Saturnian Freak" for his debut mixtape, planned to be finished late June. Two tracks, along with many songs on the project, are produced by the talented Solo Otto, complimenting Lagoon’s mellow vocals, similar to the likes of The Weeknd & Lana Del Rey, who he finds as inspiration. Presented as being smug, the lead single "Dominion" is the perfect track to become acquainted with a new artist.
