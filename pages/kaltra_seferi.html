Kaltra Seferi is an Albanian American multidisciplinary artist, she uses art as her main medium of communication. After receiving her Bachelor of Science in Architecture from the University of Illinois at Chicago in 2019, Kaltra explored the art of space through the interior vs exterior lens as an interior designer and facade designer. Over the last 21 years, Kaltra has focused on developing her own personal techniques amongst several mediums-from the 2D; photography, painting, collage to the 3D such as sculpture, textile art, and installations. Kaltra’s art is interested in exploring the alchemy of creation, the process of creating art and building, and the intersection of art and architectural practice.
